Welcome!

A character is positioned at bottom of of the canvas.
Use arrow keys of your computer (UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT) to move the character.

Move the character to get a star (worth 1 point) and/or a heart(worth 2 points) to gain points. You will win if you gain 10 points.

If you get hit by running bugs on street, you will lose 1 point. If your points become 0, game over! You have 3 points at the beginning.

Have fun!
